# FW-3 — Higher-Order Effort-Distribution Tier: Design

**Status:** Solomon-authored (Opus 4.8 / ultracode, 2026-07-10), propose-only (CONST-002). Q2 of the standing queue. Designed via the diverse-lens panel the duty contract itself prescribes (`HIGHER-ORDER EFFORT-DISTRIBUTION TIER DESIGN DUTY` → "consensus before finalizing via a diverse-lens panel") — 5 independent Opus lenses (first-principles / systems-thinking / historical-pattern / CMV-alignment / adversarial-kill), synthesized cold. **Method note:** the panel was ADVERSARIAL (each lens red-teamed the seed, one lens argued to kill the whole thing), not confirmer — which is *why* it produced load-bearing dissent. This is dogfooding: the design's central finding (below) is that the mechanism must be adversarial, and the design process that produced it validated that by being adversarial.
**Grounds:** the seed brainstorm (`docs/architecture/solomon-higher-order-effort-fleet-brainstorm.md`, Adam, ~70% seeded) + the Fable-suitability map v1 + the operating-company spine (which the seed PREDATES). **Model-pluggable by requirement:** routes by effort × abstraction regardless of pinned apex model; Fable is the apex upgrade, Opus the floor.

---

## 0. The four corrections to the seed (the panel's convergent findings)
The seed's shape (singleton **router** that **classifies on arrival** → **consensus-synthesis panel** → hand-down, with **abstraction as a second within-tier distribution dial**) is corrected on four load-bearing points, each converged across ≥4 of 5 lenses:

1. **No classify-on-arrival router.** Below-dispatch works because SDs arrive PRE-SCORED (author sets `min_tier_rank` at sourcing — score-at-*source*). Above, the unit is an *undefined problem*, so a classifier must do most of the framing just to decide how much framing to do — a meta-reasoning regress where classify ≈ solve. **The existing Solomon singleton IS the tier**; there is no standing router object.
2. **Adversarial-survival, not consensus-synthesis.** N agreeing lenses averaged into one judgment is the parallel-confirmer disease one altitude up (and you paid for N to get one adjudicator anyway). Decision quality comes from *decorrelated error*: lenses are RED-TEAMERS; a lens that BREAKS the framing is a success (it found the negative space) → re-frame. Consensus = *survival*, not averaging.
3. **Abstraction is a distinct axis but NOT a within-tier distribution dial** (proven orthogonal to Reasoning-Depth in both directions). It is the **tier-selection / apex-eligibility** property — and it collapses with two other things into ONE gate.
4. **Reconciliation with the operating-company spine = (iii) recursive-application, not a new tier.** The "effort-distribution tier" is the spine's own governance machinery applied to one role. Building it as a satellite or peer duplicates the apex objective function — the divergent-flag/SSOT defect class.

---

## 1. The two axes (resolved)
- **Reasoning-Depth** = steps-ahead / inference-chain length to REACH an answer. An INPUT property (what the problem requires). Selects EFFORT level. It is a **FLOOR** (WORK-DOWN-NEVER-UP is safe — a strong model may do shallow work).
- **Abstraction** = altitude / referent-scope of the ANSWER (implementation → component → architecture → systemic framing). An OUTPUT property (whose job). Selects WHICH TIER. It is an **EXACT BAND** — mis-altitude fails in *both* directions (a systemic problem sent down yields a precise answer to the wrong question; a concrete task sent up has no referent and burns the apex).
- **Orthogonal, proven:** high-abstraction/low-depth ("what business are we actually in — a learning-speed engine, not a venture studio" — one-leap re-frame, near-zero steps-ahead) vs low-abstraction/high-depth ("trace the exact 8-step interleave in the claim-reissue path yielding the floor-bypass double-claim" — max concreteness, deep lookahead: Opus's observed sweet spot). Different match-rule (floor vs exact-band) ⇒ provably distinct axis, not a facet.
- **The three-way collapse (the key simplification):** abstraction-detection ≡ the Impact×Opportunity **apex-eligibility gate** ≡ the empirical **Fable-breadth signatures**. All three are the same signal: *cross-system recurrence, self-reversal, negative-space, CMV-drift.* So there is ONE gate, not two, and it is exactly the Fable-differentiated axis the suitability map found. **Mechanical detector (the min_tier_rank analog):** does resolving the problem ADD LEAVES to the SD tree (concrete → below-flow) or RE-ROOT / RE-PARTITION it (abstract → apex)? And the CMV punchline (cmv lens): the top rung — acceptance defined at CMV/portfolio scope — IS the govern-by-exception boundary; abstraction-detection and chairman-escalation-detection are one mechanism viewed from two angles.

## 2. Router shape — REPLACE the seed's lean-(a) classify-on-arrival with two primitives
The singleton stands (CMV-mandated, not merely cost-preferred: one north-star ⇒ one apex framing authority — survives even if compute were free). What is deleted is the *router-as-triage-session*. Replace with:
- **P1 — SCORE-AT-ESCALATION (the correct min_tier_rank analog):** whoever escalates a problem (Adam, a stuck worker past the RCA ladder, a gate-breach *pattern*, a chairman directive) attaches the **altitude-signature + depth-floor** at escalation, exactly as an SD author sets `min_tier_rank` at sourcing. Score-at-source, never score-on-arrival. This sets the FLOOR and is what gates apex-eligibility (§1's collapsed gate). Below the bar → declines down to the normal below-flow; the decline-down gate is the single load-bearing mechanic when apex-model == worker-model (Opus today), because it is the *only* thing differentiating the apex.
- **P2 — FLOOR-FIRST SELF-ESCALATION:** the apex always starts at its floor (Opus@high), emits a framing PLUS a **residual-depth signal**, and self-escalates to higher effort (→ Fable) only if residual trips. *The floor attempt IS the depth measurement* — strictly cheaper and more accurate than an a-priori guess, and it inverts the seed's borrowed conservative-UP bias correctly (see §5: above the apex, uncertain → DOWN, because over-effort regresses). "Router" survives only as this intra-wake self-escalation, bounded by the per-sweep `task_budget` ceiling.

## 3. The panel — adversarial-survival + an INDEPENDENT rejecter
- **Lenses as red-team, run as a checklist inside one pass** (+ one optional adversarial red-team sub-call above a high Impact×Opportunity threshold — the pattern this very design used). The Vision's "expertise instantiated and COMBINED / combinatorial panels" is satisfied by combination *in one head*; it does not mandate a multi-session collection + consensus-computation + tie-break protocol. Lens set: first-principles ("is this the real root?"), systems ("what coupling does it ignore?"), historical ("when did this pattern fail before?"), + the **mandatory CMV lens** ("does this drift from the north-star?").
- **Consensus = survival.** The framing that survives every red-team ships. A lens that breaks it → re-frame (not tie-break). Only *irreducible* conflict — two mutually-exclusive valid framings — escalates, and that IS a thesis-fork = **chairman authority** (spine §5.1 exception), never an internal tie-break lens.
- **The CONST-002 correction (sharpest governance finding, cmv lens):** the mandatory CMV-alignment lens is *proposer-side*, so a framing's self-generated CMV-trace is the proposer certifying its own alignment — the exact CONST-002 violation dressed as compliance. "Every framing traces to CMV" is a **quality FLOOR masquerading as a governance CEILING.** Real approval requires an **independent rejecter, OUTSIDE the panel:** a structurally-separate adversarial CMV-rejecter (cheap-model, only job = try to REJECT on CMV grounds; adversarial-verify beats confirmer-consensus) for instrument-class framings, and the **chairman** for pick-class. And a gauge on the rejecter itself: if the downstream reject-rate ≈ 0, the separation is fake (the architecture makes Adam structurally deferential to "the bigger brain") and must flag.

## 4. Reconciliation with the operating-company spine — (iii) recursive-application
There is **no separate effort-distribution tier to reconcile.** Framing is an ACTIVITY (spine S2.1) performed by a ROLE — the **apex framing role**, a *system-scope* spine role (EVA's strategy-peer, reasoning about the system-of-ventures + the harness itself, not one venture — so NOT a venture satellite). It is governed by the identical spine invariants, and "effort-distribution" decomposes entirely into them:
- **The objective function (S3.3) MUST recurse into the spine, not fork into a satellite or peer.** CMV is *singular* — one north-star, one definition of "better" — so the apex's "better" cannot be duplicated. A satellite would be gauged by its own metric (smuggling in the cost/throughput drift, §5); a peer creates two "betters" that silently diverge across an unowned seam. Both are the duplicate-SSOT / divergent-flag defect class the spine's "nothing ungauged/unowned" principle forbids. The apex-framing objective function is **one row in the spine's §3.3 table.**
- **Only value-NEUTRAL plumbing is a satellite (i):** the transport wire (§6), the abstraction/depth detection rubric, the escalation-latency budgets. These carry no "better" and are genuinely separable.
- Net: **(iii) for the objective function (recurses into §3.3), (i) for the plumbing, never (ii) peer.** "As above, so below" done rightly = **invariant-reuse** (same governance at every altitude), not **structure-duplication** (a mirror tier with its own router/panel/wire — the very thing §2/§3 delete). This *sharpens* the earlier "lands as §3.3 + one satellite" ruling: §3.3 is the whole of the load-bearing content; the satellite is only neutral wiring.

## 5. The objective function for the apex role (the §3.3 row — and the CMV drift it must resist)
**Name and gauge it as a DECISION-QUALITY router, never an effort/cost router.** The seed's name — "effort-*distribution*" — has a gravitational pull toward "cheapest sufficient effort," a COST function that directly contradicts the Vision's *"maximize decision quality and learning speed within the capacity we have — allocate compute by expected value, never token thrift for its own sake, never waste."* Cost/capacity is a constraint (the `task_budget` ceiling, a managed fact), never the objective.

The objective function ("better" for this role):
- surfaced **negative-space** the below-flow would have missed (the apex's entire reason to exist);
- the **escalate-predicate calibrated** — false-escalations (over-framing a QF-sized problem, burning the chairman's backlog = over-escalation) AND missed-escalations (a real drift left un-framed = under-escalation) are the *symmetric* error signal, per the spine's bi-directional anti-Goodhart rule;
- the **mental-model compounded / got reused** (the learning-moat);
- it traced **honestly** to CMV (not post-hoc rationalization).
- **NOT** "which framings shipped value" — that naive RL signal *trains the apex to stop being an apex* (drifts toward throughput/easy-ship framings = the Sonnet failure elevated to the top).

**Guard gauges (anti-Goodhart pairing):** DECLINE-rate must stay HIGH (a low-decline apex is drifting — it should refuse most problems down); DISSENT-preservation-rate (a panel optimizing for agreement kills the negative-space buy). **Gauge only DOWNSTREAM** (did the hand-down produce a sourced SD that proved out), never intrinsically (framings-emitted / panel-converged / tokens-saved — the gauge-vs-action divergence at the apex).

**THE SHARPEST RISK — govern-by-exception silently degrading into govern-by-ABSENCE.** Framing is the one act *upstream of every approval gate*: choosing to frame problem P as "about X" forecloses "about Z." If framing flows Solomon→Adam→SDs→build with no fail-closed pick-class escalation and no chairman-visible framing digest, the chairman loses the most consequential strategic act — *deciding what the problem is* — while every gauge reads GREEN (traces to CMV, Adam approved, panel survived). He cannot except what he never sees. **Mitigations (both required):** (1) a **fail-closed pick-vs-instrument predicate** — a framing whose acceptance is written at CMV/portfolio altitude OR whose SDs would touch the only-the-chairman-can set is a PICK → routes to the chairman-escalate fork, NOT to Adam-sourcing; fail-closed (a framing that can't PROVE it is instrument-not-pick escalates). (2) a periodic **chairman-visible framing digest** (screen RANKS, chairman PICKS; only-the-chairman-can hold-and-surface at the apex).

## 6. The open comms questions (a)–(e), resolved
- **(a) panel transport — in-process sub-agent fan-out, and it is now a non-question:** there is no standing panel of sessions to address; the "panel" is the one floor-framing session sampling adversarial lenses in-process before self-escalating.
- **(b) collect / synthesize / break-disagreement — adversarial survival:** single adjudicator (Solomon) writes a rebuttal to each red-team; no consensus computation, no tie-break lens; irreducible conflict = a thesis-fork → chairman (§3).
- **(c) hand-down wire — REUSE `solomon_systemic_finding` + a `payload.framing_class` sub-discriminator** (confirm Adam's lean, one refinement): the sub-discriminator must be richer than `framing=true` — it is `framing_class ∈ {instrument, pick}`, because a pick-class framing mis-routed to Adam-sourcing bypasses the chairman (§5). `instrument → Adam` disposition lane; `pick → chairman-escalation`. A new `solomon_framing` kind is rejected: framing-vs-finding is a payload-SHAPE difference on the same leg with the same lifecycle (two-stage ACK), and new kinds must encode new *transport/lifecycle* semantics, never a payload shape — plus a new kind costs three allowlist edits + the `isOrphanedAdamRow` orphan-drain risk. (The through-line: reject the classify-router and confirm wire-reuse from ONE principle — never duplicate machinery to express an altitude/shape difference; reuse the invariant, add a discriminator.)
- **(d) hibernation phasing — largely DISSOLVES:** no new standing party is introduced (the framing role IS the existing Solomon singleton, already phased vs Coordinator + Adam); self-escalation floor→Fable is intra-wake, bounded by `task_budget`, so no 300s-spacing problem arises. **One addition (cmv):** the hard-park precondition must include *"no undelivered chairman-escalation outstanding"* — a tier that escalated a pick then hibernated would strand the exception (govern-by-exception with an undeliverable exception; only-the-chairman-can hold-and-surface applied to the apex).
- **(e) transport contract compliance — unchanged from the seed's §5 marching-orders** (typed kind, two-stage ACK, dual-key correlation echo, sender-side receipts, dead-letter-never-delete, META-RULE announce-and-document); those port verbatim.

## 7. Verification plan (mandatory — high blast radius, this touches apex governance)
1. **The calibration-loop-breaks test (historical lens's warning):** below-dispatch self-tunes on a high-volume / clean / short-delay ground truth (gate-pass, rework counts); the apex framing outcome is low-volume / long-delayed / confounded. Before wiring any bidirectional effort-tuning, PROVE a closable signal exists — else the tuning has nothing to learn on and a "mirror the below" design over-frames *and cannot learn that it did*. If no closable signal: ship the decline-gate + honest-trace + downstream-gauge, defer auto-tuning explicitly.
2. **Pick-vs-instrument fail-closed test:** a framing implying kill/scale/thesis-reversal MUST route to chairman-escalation, not Adam-sourcing; a framing that cannot prove instrument-class escalates. Seed a thesis-reversal framing and assert it never reaches an auto-source path.
3. **Rejecter-reality test:** measure Adam's disposition reject-rate on instrument framings; reject-rate ≈ 0 flags fake separation (structural deference), per §3.
4. **Govern-by-absence test:** assert a chairman-visible framing digest exists and fires on cadence — the chairman must be able to see *what problems were framed and how* even when every downstream gauge is green.
5. **Model-pluggability test:** the routing/detection rubric must run identically with Fable pinned at the apex; confirm no logic hardcodes Opus-as-apex (Fable-swap is a config change, per the seed's model-strategy requirement).

**Counterfactual (per §7 output contract — and it materially shaped this design):** the adversarial-kill lens argued FW-3 is over-engineering — that the suitability map + the existing Solomon + Adam's escalation *already are* the tier, and a router+panel+hibernation apparatus adds failure surface for near-zero marginal value. **That case largely WON:** the final design has no standing router (existing Solomon is it), no standing consensus panel (adversarial checklist in one pass), and no hibernation-phasing apparatus (dissolved). What survived its attack — and only what survived: the decline-down GATE (re-justified as apex-*differentiation*, not token-saving), the honest CMV-trace as a required output field, the fail-closed pick-vs-instrument predicate, the independent rejecter, and wire-reuse. Everything that is "standing infrastructure with a lifecycle" was cut; only "doctrine + one pure eligibility function + reused wire + a spine §3.3 row" remains.

**CONFIDENCE:** high on the architecture (5-lens convergence, adversarially stress-tested, grounded in the spine + suitability map). Medium on the residual-depth-signal + altitude-signature *detection mechanics* (the one place a Fable pass would sharpen instrument design — but the class-level shape is settled; this is Opus-adequate for the architecture, per the map's own tiering). **ESCALATE_TO_CHAIRMAN:** no for the design — but the design's own core is that pick-class framings escalate to him, so the mechanism embeds the escalation it describes.

*Propose-only. Owner: the apex-framing role = a spine §3.3 row (recursive-application). Adam sources the coupled work (a spine §3.3 amendment + the neutral-plumbing satellite) after the coordinator accuracy check; nothing here is a standalone tier.*
