# Demand-Thesis Adjudication Frame (S5-class) — Phase-1 posture

**Status:** Solomon-authored (Fable, 2026-07-10), propose-only (CONST-002). The reusable checklist Solomon applies when a Discovery Mode pick arrives for demand-thesis adjudication — pre-built so adjudication is same-hour. Doubles as the S5 half of the kill-gate-semantics territory (deep-challenge commission overflow). Verdict vocabulary and evidence discipline inherit the venture-selection/demand-thesis design (2026-07-07) and the Stage-0 greenfield spec (R2/R3/R4).

## Frame constants (this round)
- **Posture:** Phase-1 process-proving (chairman-ratified): simplicity dominant; **full-26-stage traversability is a hard criterion**; revenue = tiebreaker only; anti-goals auto-disqualify (long sales cycles, content moats, app-store surface, regulatory surface).
- **Adjudicator independence:** the pick's own synthesis scores are INPUTS TO RE-DERIVE, never premises (D2). The engine just had its false-green class fixed — trust the canary, not the composite.

## The checklist (verdict per item; weakest-link governs)

**A. Thesis falsifiability (R3):** Is there an actual thesis — *who pays, for what, how reached, at what price*? If any of the four is missing or generic ("SMBs", "via marketing"), the thesis is NOT ADJUDICABLE — return for thesis completion before demand judgment (do not adjudicate vibes).

**B. Evidence grades (R4, E0–E3 per load-bearing claim):**
- Demand claim (people want this): grade + source. LLM-asserted market appetite = E0.
- Reachability claim (we can put it in front of them): grade + source. A named channel the factory can actually execute (O9 envelope) or E0.
- Price claim (they'd pay $X): grade + source. Van-Westendorp-style elicitation or real comparable = E2+; invented bands = E0.
- **Weakest-link rule:** the thesis grade = min of the three. E0 overall → the demand test IS the next step, never the build.

**C. Stranger-pays discipline:** Would a stranger — not the chairman, not an agent — plausibly pay within the process-proving window? The test plan must name the *first stranger path* (where the first uninvolved human encounters it) and what they do that costs them something (money, signup, time). "Stranger pays TWICE" (returns/renews) is the strong form; Phase-1 requires at least the weak form be *testable pre-build*.

**D. Pre-registered kills (R3):** Are kill criteria registered with stage + threshold ("dies if no X by stage Y")? Each must be *evaluable by a gauge that will actually exist at that stage* (O4 provenance-reached — a kill criterion on a gauge that renders NO-DATA is decorative). Minimum: one demand kill (pre-build test fails), one traversal kill (stage progress stalls), one economics kill (cost exceeds cap).

**E. Phase-1 posture conformance (R2):** Simplicity check (scope small enough for fast full-traversal); anti-goal scan (auto-disqualify on hit — no exceptions without chairman waiver); traversability check against the capability envelope (every stage the venture needs, the factory can genuinely run — incl. the S20-26 operating loops per the cluster-2 spec; a venture that can't be *operated* fails Phase-1's whole point).

**F. Demand-test plan before build:** Concrete, cheap, time-boxed, with a declared pass/fail threshold — executable with test-mode containment (no real spend without chairman authority). The plan's outcome feeds B's grades; the venture builds only on a passing test or an explicit chairman waiver recorded as a disposition.

## Output contract (per §7)
`recommendation` ∈ {PROCEED-TO-DEMAND-TEST | PROCEED-TO-BUILD (only if B ≥ E2 already) | RETURN-FOR-THESIS-COMPLETION | DISQUALIFY (anti-goal/traversability) } + why (per-item verdicts) + counterfactual (what evidence would flip it) + the pre-registered kills restated + confidence + escalate_to_chairman only if the pick collides with a chairman-only matter. The chairman PICKS; this adjudication advises the pick's demand-thesis, never overrides it.
