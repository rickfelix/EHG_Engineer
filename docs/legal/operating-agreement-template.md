## ARTICLE II - SERIES DESIGNATION

### Section 2.1 - Series LLC Status
The Company is organized as a Series LLC pursuant to Section 18-215 of the Act. The debts, liabilities, obligations, and expenses of one Series shall be enforceable only against the assets of that Series and not against the assets of any other Series or the Master LLC.

### Section 2.2 - Series Creation
New Series may be created by the Manager(s) through:
1. Adoption of a Series Designation document
2. Recording the Series in the Company's Series Register
3. Maintaining separate records and accounts for the Series

### Section 2.3 - Series Naming Convention
Each Series shall be named: "[COMPANY NAME] LLC - [SERIES NAME] Series"

Example: "EHG Holdings LLC - Venture Alpha Series"
