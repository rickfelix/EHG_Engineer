## Executive Summary

All three AIs reached **strong consensus** on the primary blind spot: **Multi-Venture Portfolio Management (EVA)** must be addressed first. Without an operating system for the holding company, EHG will hit the "Management Cliff" at 8-12 ventures.

**Key Consensus Points**:
- EVA is the #1 priority (unanimous)
- Decentralized operations with centralized metrics (Constellation model)
- Series LLC is recommended legal structure
- Pattern deprecation is lowest priority early on
- "Management by Exception" is the operational philosophy

| Rank | OpenAI | Gemini | Claude Code |
|------|--------|--------|-------------|

### 2.4 A/B Testing Pricing

**Resolution**:
