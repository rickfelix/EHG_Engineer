## Open Questions for Triangulation

### Strategic Questions:

1. **Capability Ledger**: How do we formally track which capabilities exist and which venture produced them? Schema design?

2. **Capability ROI Metric**: How do we measure the "ecosystem lift" of a capability? Usage count? Time saved? Ventures accelerated?

3. **Venture Admission Rule**: Should we reject ventures that don't add a reusable capability? Or just deprioritize them?

4. **Capability Injection Mechanism**: How does EVA automatically inject capabilities into new ventures? API? Database? Agent memory?

5. **Capability vs. Feature**: What distinguishes a "capability" (reusable primitive) from a "feature" (one-time implementation)?

### Implementation Questions:

6. **Where in the 25-Stage Workflow**: At which stage should "Secondary Output: Capability" be defined and extracted?

7. **Capability Discovery**: For existing/past ventures, how do we retroactively identify capabilities they produced?

8. **Capability Deprecation**: When a capability becomes obsolete, how is it retired?

9. **Capability Conflicts**: What if two ventures produce competing capabilities for the same function?

10. **Capability Quality Gate**: Who certifies a capability as "reusable" vs. "one-off hack"?
