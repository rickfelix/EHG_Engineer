## Problem Statement
EHG has 8 active ventures with no centralized content production capability. Marketing materials, TikTok videos, and promotional content are produced ad-hoc. A standalone AI multimedia venture could serve as both a new business AND a shared service for the portfolio.

## Discovery Summary
- Chairman wants a standalone venture (not just a service) with fully autonomous agent production
- LTX Desktop is a candidate tool: free, open-source AI video editor with timeline + generation
- Hardware constraint: 32GB VRAM needed, Mac insufficient for local gen, cloud GPU required
- Production model: AI agent receives brief → produces finished content autonomously

## Analysis

### Arguments For
- Cross-venture content flywheel — performance data improves targeting across portfolio
- LTX is free/open-source — fork potential for proprietary editor
- Content as a capability in EVA pipeline (agent-to-agent handoff)
- 8 ventures = built-in customer base

### Arguments Against
- Cloud GPU costs ($200-500/month) may exceed freelancer rates
- Fully autonomous creative content is bleeding edge — quality risk
- Solo entrepreneur adding 9th venture to full portfolio
- Internal ventures may resist paying market rates (cost center risk)

## Team Perspectives

### Challenger
- Blind Spots: Internal margins (cost center vs venture), AI output consistency at scale, hidden ops costs
- Assumptions at Risk: "Fully autonomous in Year 1", content briefs being detailed enough
- Worst Case: 3 months building agent that produces mediocre clips, $2K/month compute, becomes body shop

### Visionary
- Opportunities: Content distribution network effect, agent-to-agent handoff layer, LTX+EVA integration
- Synergies: Claim/handoff system extends to multimedia, retrospective learning feeds positioning
- Upside: 16-32 monthly assets, pattern recognition across portfolio, licensing to external portfolios

### Pragmatist
- Feasibility: 6/10
- Resources: 4-6 weeks MVP, cloud GPU, LTX API, Supabase job queue
- Constraints: Mac hardware gap, LTX beta maturity, agent complexity for creative work
- Path: Prototype with cloud API (Week 1-2), build job queue (Week 3), integrate LEO (Week 4-6)
